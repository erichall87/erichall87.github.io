I'm Eric Hall, a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Wisconsin-Madison working with Professor Rebecca Willett. My research has focused primarily on the analysis of time-evolving signals in dynamic environments, specifically with relation to online convex optimization and autoregressive point processes. My interests include machine learning, network theory, image processing, and numerical optimization.

I received the Bachelors of Science in Engineering in Electrical and Computer Engineering with a second major in Mathematics from Duke in 2010, and the Masters of Science degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Duke in 2013, and the PhD in ECE in 2015 from Duke.

I have recently accepted a Data Scientist position at the exciting, and rapidly growing startup Uptake.
