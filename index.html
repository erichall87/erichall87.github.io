I'm Eric Hall, a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Duke University and a visiting researcher at the University of Wisconsin-Madison working with Professor Rebecca Willett. My research has focused primarily on the analysis of time-evolving signals in dynamic environments, specifically with relation to online convex optimization and autoregressive point processes.

I received the Bachelors of Science in Engineering in Electrical and Computer Engineering with a second major in Mathematics from Duke in 2010, and the Masters of Science degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Duke in 2013.
